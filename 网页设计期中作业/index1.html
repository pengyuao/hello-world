Huangshan: the world cultural and natural heritage, the world geopark, the national AAAAA level tourist attraction, the national scenic spot, the national civilized scenic tourist area demonstration site, China's top ten famous mountains, the world's best mountain. Huangshan Mountain is located in Huangshan City, southern Anhui Province. It has 72 peaks, the main one of which is Lianhua Peak 1,864 meters above sea level. Together with Bright Summit and Tiandu Peak, it is one of the three major peaks of Huangshan Mountain. Huangshan is the symbol of Anhui tourism. Huangshan Mountain was originally named "Black Mountain", and it is famous for its black peaks and black rocks and the dark sky. After the legend Xuan Yuan Huang Emperor had alchemy in this, so it was renamed "Huangshan Mountain." The representative landscapes of Huangshan are "Five Wondrous Pines and Three Waterfalls", five wonders: strange pines, grotesque rocks, sea of clouds, hot springs and winter snow; Three waterfalls: herringbone waterfall, Baizhang spring, and Jiulong waterfall. The Yellow Mountain Visiting Pine is a symbol of the warmth and friendship of the people of Anhui and carries the Oriental etiquette culture of embracing the world. Xu Xiake, a traveler in the Ming Dynasty, praised Huangshan: "There is no mountain as famous as Huangshan, both inside and outside the Bo Hai. Climb Huangshan Mountain, there are no mountains in the world. It was extended by later generations as "the five mountains do not see the return of the mountain, Huangshan do not see the return of the mountain".
